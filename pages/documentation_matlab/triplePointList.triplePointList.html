grainBoundary list of grain boundaries in 2-D

grainBoundary is used to extract, analyze and visualize grain boundaries in 2-D.

gB = grainBoundary() creates an empty list of grain boundaries

gB = grains.boudary() extracts boundary information from a list of grains
