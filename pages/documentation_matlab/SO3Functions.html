The SO3Fun toolbox is a collection of Matlab classes for numerical computations with functions on the rotation group \(\mathcal{SO}(3)\). It overloads the default commands for vectors and matrices to compute the analogous operations for functions of the given type. The underlying mathematical approach is accomplished via Wigner-D functions which form an orthonormal basis of the square-integrable functions on the rotation group.
