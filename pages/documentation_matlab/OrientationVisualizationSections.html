Sections of the orientations space

A third possibility are two dimensional sections through the Euler angle space. The most popular type of such sections are the so called phi2 sections.

{% highlight matlab %} % cubic crystal symmetry cs = crystalSymmetry('432'); % orthotropic specimen symmetry ss = specimenSymmetry('222'); % 100 random orientations ori = orientation.rand(100,cs,ss) % plotted as as phi2 sections plotSection(ori,'phi2') {% endhighlight %} {% highlight plaintext %} ori = orientation size: 100 x 1 crystal symmetry : 432 specimen symmetry: 222 {% endhighlight %}

Sigma Sections

A different type of sections through the orientation space are the so called sigma sections. One of its central advantages is its equal volume projection.

{% highlight matlab %} plotSection(ori,'sigma') {% endhighlight %}
