Unimodal ODFs

An unimodal ODF

\[f(g; x) = \psi (\angle(g,x)),\quad g \in SO(3),\]

is specified by a radially symmetrial function \(\psi\) centered at a modal orientation, \(x\in SO(3)\) and. In order to define a unimodal ODF one needs

  • a preferred orientation mod1
  • a kernel function psi defining the shape
  • the crystal symmetry
