--- title: ellipsoid last_updated: 07-Oct-2019 sidebar: function_reference_sidebar permalink: strainTensor.ellipsoid.html folder: function_reference toc: false --- ellipsoidedit page

(method of strainTensor)

Length (1+e) and direction of principal axes of strain ellipsoid from finite, lagrangian/eulerian strain tensor E with eigenvalues lambda with (1+e) = sqrt(1+2*lambda) for a Lagranage strain tensor (default) (1+e) = sqrt(1-2*lambda) for a Euler strain tensor

Syntax

[fe,qe] = ellipsoid(E)

Input

E <a href="strainTensor_index.html">strainTensor</a>

Output

fe directions of prinicpal axes of strain ellipsoid <a href="vector3d_index.html">vector3d</a>
qe length of ellipse axes (1+e)
