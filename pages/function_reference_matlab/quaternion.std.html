mean angular deviation

Syntax

[m, lambda, V] = std(q)
[m, lambda, V] = std(q,'robust')
[m, lambda, V] = std(q,'weights',weights)

Input

q list of [[quaternion_index.html,quaternion]]
weights list of weights

Output

m mean orientation
lambda principle moments of inertia
V principle axes of inertia ([[quaternion_index.html,quaternion]])
