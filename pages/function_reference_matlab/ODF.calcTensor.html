compute the average tensor for an ODF

Syntax

{% highlight matlab %} [TVoigt, TReuss, THill] = calcTensor(odf,T) THill = calcTensor(odf,T,'Hill') TGeo = calcTensor(odf,T,'geometric') {% endhighlight %}

Input

odf ODF
T tensor

Output

T tensor

Options

Voigt Boigt mean
Reuss Reuss mean
Hill Hill mean
geometric geometric mean
