(method of Miller)

define a crystal direction by Miller indice

Syntax

{% highlight matlab %} m = Miller(h,k,l,cs) m = Miller(h,k,l,cs,'hkl') m = Miller(h,k,l,cs,'pole') m = Miller(h,k,i,l,cs) m = Miller('(hkl)',cs) m = Miller(u,v,w,cs,'uvw') m = Miller(u,v,t,w,cs,'uvw') m = Miller(u,v,w,cs,'direction') m = Miller('[uvw]',cs) m = Miller('[uvw]\[uvw],cs) m = Miller('(hkl)\(hkl),cs) m = Miller(x,cs) % transform vector3d to Miller {% endhighlight %}

Input

h,k,l,i Miller indice of the plane normal
u,v,w,t Miller indice of a direction
x vector3d
cs crystal symmetry
